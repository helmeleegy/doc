Help¶

Please feel free to reach out to us to give feedback, get help, chat and ask questions about Ponder. We love hearing from our customers and community.


How can I submit a bug report, feature request, or other question?

To submit a bug/issue or request a feature please send an email to support@ponder.io and include following information

  1. Email subject

    Example Bug subject: “Bug: Timeout/Connection Error when loading large file”

    Example Feature Request subject: “Feature: enable users to upload files to S3”

  2. Describe the issue/bug or requested feature in the email body.

  3. If applicable, explain the steps to reproduce the bug or add screenshots

Note

You can also browse the FAQ for answers to your questions. Any security-related concerns can be reported to security@ponder.io
