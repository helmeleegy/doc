	<p>Please feel free to reach out to us to give feedback, get help, chat and ask questions about Ponder.  We love hearing from our customers and community. </p> </br>

	<h4 style="line-height: 0; padding: 0.6px;">How can I submit a bug report, feature request, or other question?</h4> 
	To submit a bug/issue or request a feature please send an email to <a href="mailto:support@ponder.io">support@ponder.io</a> and include following information
1. Email subject
    
    **Example Bug subject:** 
    `“Bug: Timeout/Connection Error when loading large file”` 

    **Example Feature Request subject:** 
    `“Feature: enable users to upload files to S3”`
    
2. Describe the issue/bug or requested feature in the email body. 
3. If applicable, explain the steps to reproduce the bug or add screenshots



.. note:: 
  | You can also browse the FAQ for answers to your questions. Any security-related concerns can be reported to security@ponder.io
